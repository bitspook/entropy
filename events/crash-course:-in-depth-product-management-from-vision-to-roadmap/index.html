Products are a solution to the needs/problems of a set of users. In order to build the products which customers love and business happy, we have to make sure we follow certain tools and techniques. In this crash course, we will explore some of those techniques. We will start off with framing vision then get introduced to understanding users through user journey and build a product roadmap.
