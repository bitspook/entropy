Leadership Agility growth involves awareness, consistent practice and regular reflection of the progress. We also need triggers that could help remember this rhythm. Developing more Adaptive actions starts with building awareness of the default actions. Most times, they are impacted by cognitive threats. Leaders also impact others by inducing threats. Once aware, we could practice more adaptive actions through co-creative conversations.
