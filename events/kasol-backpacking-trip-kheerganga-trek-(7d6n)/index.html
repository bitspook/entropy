Hola Backpackers!

Kasol is a paradise where all the birds with backpacking feathers will fly down to. Kasol is a picturesque village in Kullu, stretched along river Parvati is in every trekker’s checklist. There are abundant treks and hikes that take you to absolutely stunning villages hidden far from civilization in their own world and time.

This backpacking trip doesn’t leave any stone unturned not just in Kasol, but also in Tosh, Kheerganga, and Malana.

Come join us!
