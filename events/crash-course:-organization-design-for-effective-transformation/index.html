Organization Design involves creating the creation of roles, processes, structures, and policies to help the organization achieve its goals. It is the primary step towards Product Transformation. If not done right it may lead to Suboptimization and adding waste. In this crash course, we will discuss how Organization design can help with an effective organizational transformation.

Role of Organization Design in Product Transformation
Once you define the Product Taxonomy, the very next step is to organize as per Product and Product Boundaries. This will help define the Product Backlog and primary roles. The team structure and defining feature-based teams. Organization Design is a continuous process as Products are being defined and matures, your structures may evolve over time.

Systems Thinking and Organization Design
Thinking as a whole using system thinking is missed by many leaders and requires practice, and awareness. Making Organization as lean and flat as possible is primary to real transformation.

Tools and tips on how to go about Org Design
Through this course, you will learn about the importance of Organization Design, Feature-based teams, Products, and Components. Some of the tools and tips on how to go about defining the structure which will make your teams and organization fit for purpose.
