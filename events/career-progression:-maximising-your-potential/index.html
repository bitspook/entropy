ðŸ’¡ Topic of Discussion
This 90-minute webinar is focused broadly on the thinking that is needed in the contemporary environment, and identifying those opportunities that will fast-track your career advancement. Determining the specific combination of your skills you need to acquire, behaviours you need to adopt and the broad-based thinking to advance your career or take it in a different direction can be a daunting challenge. The aim is also to provide career conversation tips for both the leaders and their team members for meaningful career development plans.

At the end of the session, participants will:
- Be able to learn a number of proven career strategies to adapt to the fast-changing workplace and business dynamics
- Redesign their career plans to garner maximum impact.
- Be able to conceptualise and prioritise their growth areas to meet their career objectives.
- Learn how to overcome the biggest blockers and barriers to career advancement.
- Get the most out of career development conversations.

Curated with analogies and real-life examples, the webinar will be of interest to corporate and self-employed professionals at every level of leadership contemplating their career growth plans and seeking to maximise their opportunities and potential.
